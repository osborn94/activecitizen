Advocating for the
New Nigeria

Join us in a historic movement to achieving the new Nigeria through active participation. For too long we have been docile and our voices silenced. Let’s rise together, united in purpose and strength.

MISSION

To empower and unite young Nigerians by fostering political consciousness and active citizenship, thereby driving grassroots initiatives and holding the government accountable to ensure a better Nigeria.

VISION

To create a politically conscious and active youth community that transforms Nigeria into a nation of transparent governance, equitable opportunities, and social justice through active advocacy.

VALUES

Empower young Nigerians for leadership and civic responsibility. Advocate for transparency and good governance. Support community projects. Educate on rights and political participation. Build a national network of engaged youth. Ensure free and fair elections.
